# Vocabulary App

Expand your lexicon with a daily word, riddle, psychology fact, or joke! This app is designed to make learning new words engaging, interactive, and fun.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Word of the Day
* **Daily Learning**: Receive a daily notification introducing a new vocabulary word.
* **Definitions & Pronunciations**: View standard dictionary definitions, correct pronunciations, parts of speech, and phonetic transcriptions.

### 2. Special AI Notification Modes
Choose how you want your daily word delivered! Toggle one or more modes in the settings to customize your experience. If multiple modes are enabled, the app will randomly choose one each day:
* **🧩 Riddle Mode**: Receive an intriguing daily riddle about the word of the day. Tap the notification to reveal the word and its definition.
* **🧠 Psychology Facts Mode**: Learn a fascinating, educational psychology fact that incorporates or explains the vocabulary word.
* **🎈 Joke Mode**: Start your day with a lighthearted, clean joke designed around the daily word.
* **🖤 Dark Joke Mode**: For fans of gallows humor, receive a witty, dark humor joke featuring the vocabulary word.

### 3. Immediate Notification Triggers
* **Instant Fetch**: Don't want to wait for the scheduled time? Tap the notification bell in the app to instantly trigger a word, riddle, psychology fact, or joke notification on your device.

### 4. Custom Delivery Schedule
* **Set Your Time**: Pick the exact hour and minute you want to receive your daily notification. The app guarantees delivery precisely at your chosen time without drifting.

### 5. Smart Connection Fallbacks
* **Always Reliable**: If you are offline or if AI services are unavailable, the app automatically falls back to web-scraped dictionary examples, ensuring you never miss a daily word.
